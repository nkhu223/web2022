About Us

Here at Wishful Whiskers, our team believes in giving every cat and kitten a second chance at life. That is why we are so proud to volunteer our time to making sure that every stray cat in the Lexington area has a place to call home.


Our organization began in 2022 when the founder, Natalie Hutchinson, realized that there was a stray cat problem in her neighborhood. She decided to open up her very own shelter in memoriam of all the stray cats she has taken in throughout her life. Now, she is the founder of the Whishful Whiskers organization and the manager of the Whishful Whiskers website.
